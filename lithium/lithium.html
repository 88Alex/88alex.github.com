The Lithium-OS Project

Lithium-OS is a simple 32-bit console-based operating system for the x86 architecture.

Lithium-OS was designed to be simple and easy-to-use, but actually useful.

Principles

In the modern world, there are 2 types of operating systems- the mega-powerful yet bloated major operating systems (i.e. Windows, Mac) and extremely simple systems in which you can't even print a document. Lithium-OS attempts a compromise between the two.
